### OpenIPC Firmware
Copy the content of the following archive to an sdard:
- [foscam-openipc.zip][1]

Update your wireless credentials on autostart.sh:
```diff
#!/bin/sh
+WLAN_SSID="Router"
+WLAN_PASS="12345678"
```
